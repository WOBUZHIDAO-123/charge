XO Tactile Voice Power Bank

Product Overview

Accessibility-Focused Design, Non-Visual Power Check

Front View

The lower front features a tactile brand logo, with the tactile brand name 'WUJIE' above it. The upper-right corner contains four LED battery indicators showing the remaining battery level, while a fifth green LED indicates fast charging. The upper-left side has a multifunction button for checking battery level and switching operating modes.

Top View

From left to right: 4-dot tactile battery indicator, speaker, USB-C port, speaker, and USB-A port. Dual speakers provide clear voice announcements, while dual charging ports support multiple devices.

Tactile Battery Indicator

The four tactile dots are the core accessibility feature. By feeling the number of raised dots, users can independently identify the remaining battery level in both quiet and noisy environments.

Button Functions

  • Single Press: Check battery level
  • Double Press: Turn voice prompts on/off (Silent Mode)
  • Long Press: Switch between Voice Mode, Tactile Mode, and Voice + Tactile Mode

Tactile Power Prompt

Under dot mode and VoiceOver + dot dual mode, tap the button to get battery level reminder:

  • 4 dots: 75–100% power remaining
  • 3 dots: 50–75% power remaining
  • 2 dots: 25–50% power remaining
  • 1 dot: 0–25% power remaining

Voice Prompts

  • Start charge
  • Start discharge
  • Battery Full
  • Battery Low
  • Please charge
  • Remaining Battery Percentage

Product Specifications

  • Battery Capacity: 10,000mAh
  • Maximum Fast Charging Output: 22.5W
  • USB-C Port: Support Input & Output
  • USB-A Port: Support Output Only
  • Product Dimensions: 145 × 73 × 16 mm
  • Net Weight: 208 g
